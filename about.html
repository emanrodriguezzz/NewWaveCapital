Who We Are

New Wave Capital is a student-run hedge fund launching at the University of Michigan in Fall 2025. We’re building a dynamic space for student investors to apply high-level finance skills to real market problems.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bridge the gap between classroom theory and institutional investing. We simulate real hedge fund operations—from sector analysis to trade execution—while promoting autonomy, rigor, and ambition.
