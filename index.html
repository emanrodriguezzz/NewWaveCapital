Who We Are

Founded by Michigan students with experience at firms like BlackRock and Morgan Stanley, we offer real-world exposure to hedge fund strategy through competitive collaboration and mentorship.

Our Philosophy

We believe in creative freedom, academic rigor, and performance-based evaluation. Members can pitch anything—from options on oil to AI-driven signals—so long as there’s conviction and a clear hedge.
