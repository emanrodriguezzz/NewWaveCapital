What is New Wave Capital?

New Wave Capital is a student-led investment club at the University of Michigan, focused on market research, portfolio strategy, and financial innovation. Our members drive cross-functional research in equities, macroeconomics, and quantitative modeling to reshape student-led investing.

Our Teams

Macro Strategy

Rate cycles, policy shifts, and emerging global narratives that shape markets.

Quantitative Models

Python-based modeling, predictive algorithms, and data-driven strategies.
