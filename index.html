Who We Are

New Wave Capital is a student-run hedge fund at the University of Michigan. Founded by students with experience at top firms such as Morgan Stanley, BlackRock, and the Global Investments Committee, the club offers hands-on exposure to professional-grade investing.

What We Believe

We think differently. Our fund explores alpha through both traditional and experimental strategies — from long/short equities and private markets to macro catalysts, AI quant modeling, and polymarket arbitrage.

Investment Strategy Desks

Long/Short Equity

Fundamentally driven public equity positions with hedged downside.

Quantitative

Statistical arbitrage, algorithmic models, and backtested strategies.

Private Equity

Valuation modeling and diligence on private market opportunities.

Macro & Policy

Positioning for rate moves, elections, policy shifts, and global trends.

Arbitrage

Short-term mispricing across assets, platforms, and geopolitical events.

Wildcard

Anything outside the box — polymarkets, betting markets, AI trades.

What a Semester Looks Like

  • Desk Pitch: Each desk presents a full investment thesis and risk plan once per semester.
  • New Member Pitch: Each member completes an original investment pitch to develop technical and creative skill.
  • Education: Weekly modules on financial modeling, valuation, hedge fund strategy, and macroeconomics.
  • Culture: We host social events, career sessions, and celebrate ideas with a semester-end dinner.
  • Careers: Resume & interview workshops, alumni networking, and mentorship support.
