A Hedge Fund Club for students at the University of Michigan

Who We Are

Started by students with experience at top firms and clubs such as Morgan Stanley, BlackRock, and the Global Investments Committee.

We are a community of ambitious and high-performing students, aspiring to lead the next generation of investment analysis.

Whether you're pursuing investment banking, private equity, portfolio and wealth management, quantitative analysis, consulting, or simply curious about understanding financial markets — we offer a place to gain experience pitching and receive elite finance education.

We welcome both Ross and Non-Ross students and encourage alternative investing methods rooted in STEM and public policy.

We think outside the box. NOTHING is off limits.

Our Investment Philosophy

We aim to simulate the alpha generation of a real hedge fund with a diversified and wide range of investments—offering typical lower-risk long/short positions while allowing more creative, higher-risk pitches using everything from commodities and options trading to polymarket arbitrage and AI algorithms.

What Does a Semester Look Like?

  • Members are divided into investment desks by strategy:
    • Long/Short Equity
    • Private Equity
    • Quant, Arbitrage & Tech-Driven Strategies
    • Macro, Policy & Betting Markets
    • Alternative Long/Short (commodities, REITs, distressed debt, bonds, options)
    • Wildcard Desk (experimental strategies)
  • Each desk pitches once per semester, with a core idea and hedge plan
  • New members complete an individual pitch project
  • Weekly education sessions on hedge fund strategies and modeling
  • Semester Dinner to celebrate pitches and accomplishments
  • Club Party to build camaraderie outside finance
  • Career development: resumes, networking, and interview prep

Interested?

We're looking for people with unique views on analysis who aren't afraid to think differently and invest unconventionally.
