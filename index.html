About Us

New Wave Capital is a student-led investment organization committed to developing market intuition, equity research skills, and macroeconomic understanding. We provide hands-on investment education and sector-based team research.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a rigorous, collaborative, and intellectually curious environment for students interested in financial markets, asset management, and long-term capital allocation.
