Who We Are

Founded by students with experience at top firms like Morgan Stanley and BlackRock, New Wave is a selective investment club training the next generation of high-performing analysts.

We foster a creative and technically rigorous approach to markets — encouraging unconventional analysis, interdisciplinary thinking, and practical experience.

Investment Strategy

Long/Short Equity

Fundamental and technical equity analysis with hedged exposures.

Quant & Arbitrage

Factor-driven models, arbitrage, betting markets, and pricing dislocations.

Alternative Assets

Options, distressed debt, commodities, REITs, and experimental trades.
