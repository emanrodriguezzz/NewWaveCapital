New Wave Capital Investments

University of Michigan's Premier Student Hedge Fund Club

Who We Are

Founded by students with experience at Morgan Stanley, BlackRock, and the Global Investments Committee, New Wave Capital is an elite investment club at the University of Michigan. We develop the next generation of investors through live portfolio experience, desk collaboration, and unconventional thinking.

Our Investment Desks

Long/Short Equity

Fundamentals-first equity investing with hedge design.

Quant & Arbitrage

Statistical arbitrage, algorithmic strategies, and price misalignments.

Macro & Policy

Geopolitical impact, interest rates, policy shifts, and macro sentiment.

Alternative Assets

Options, REITs, distressed debt, private equity modeling.

Wildcard

Creative high-risk strategies including crypto, AI, and prediction markets.

The Semester Experience

  • Weekly training in hedge fund strategy and financial modeling
  • Investment desks pitch one core idea + hedge each semester
  • New members complete an individual creative pitch project
  • Semester-end dinner and strategy showcase
  • Career prep: resume sessions, interview prep, alumni network

Join the Next Wave

Recruitment opens each semester. All UM students welcome — Ross, LSA, Engineering, Public Policy, and more.
