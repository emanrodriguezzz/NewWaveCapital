Who We Are

Founded by University of Michigan students with experience at firms like BlackRock and Morgan Stanley, New Wave Capital provides practical exposure to hedge fund strategy through mentorship and competition.

Our Philosophy

We believe people learn best when the stakes are real. That’s why we focus on performance under pressure and give members freedom to invest as they see fit — with one goal: outperform the S&P 500.
