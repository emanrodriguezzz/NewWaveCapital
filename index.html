Who We Are

Founded by students with experience at Morgan Stanley, BlackRock, and the Global Investments Committee, New Wave Capital is a community of ambitious investors. We welcome Ross and non-Ross students alike, and embrace both traditional and unconventional strategies — from equity research to polymarket arbitrage.

Our Investment Desks

Long/Short Equity

Classic hedged investing via deep sector research

Private Equity

Buyout theses, LBO models, and strategic sourcing

Quant & Tech

Machine learning, signals, and algorithmic trading

Macro & Policy

Betting on interest rates, regimes, and global shifts

Alternative Assets

Commodities, distressed debt, crypto, and options

Wildcard Desk

Polymarkets, AI arbitrage, and unfiltered creativity

What a Semester Looks Like

  • ✔️ Members are assigned to a specialized desk
  • ✔️ Each desk pitches a primary thesis and hedge plan
  • ✔️ New members complete an individualized pitch project
  • ✔️ Weekly education: modeling, valuation, and strategy
  • ✔️ Semester Dinner and Party to build community
  • ✔️ Resume workshops, interview prep, and alumni support
