Pitch Competition

At the heart of New Wave Capital is our $500 Pitch Prize — an ultra-competitive semesterly challenge where members present high-conviction, professional-grade investment theses across any strategy or asset class.

Each semester, all new and returning members are invited to compete in a live pitch competition judged by alumni, faculty, and experienced finance professionals. The winning pitch not only receives $500 in prize money, but also recognition across our internal portfolio and social platforms.

Whether you're passionate about public equities, quant strategies, or frontier arbitrage, this is your moment to think independently, pitch aggressively, and win big.
