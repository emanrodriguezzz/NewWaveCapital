Built Like a Real Fund

New Wave Capital offers students an authentic hedge fund simulation. Members pitch across desks, manage hypothetical capital allocation, and compete to win a $500 prize each semester for best investment strategy.

How It Works

Strategy Desks

Members are placed on desks like Long/Short Equity, Macro, Quant, and Arbitrage. Each team delivers one professional-level pitch per semester with a hedge included.

Pitch Competition

Each semester concludes with a high-stakes pitch competition. The top team wins a $500 cash prize and is honored at the final investment committee dinner.
