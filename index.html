Who We Are

Founded by students with experience at top firms including Morgan Stanley and BlackRock, New Wave Capital offers hands-on exposure to real-world investment strategy and hedge fund operations.

Our Philosophy

We promote innovative thinking across strategies—from long/short equity to AI quant models and macroeconomic catalysts. Our fund simulates real research, portfolio construction, and risk-adjusted returns.

Investment Strategy Desks

Members collaborate in specialized desks to generate alpha across diverse strategies.

Long/Short Equity

Public equities with technical/fundamental theses and active hedging.

Quantitative

Backtested, statistically rigorous, data-driven investing models.

Macro & Policy

Policy, interest rate, and geopolitical strategy formulation.

Private Equity

Valuation modeling, due diligence, and industry analysis.

Arbitrage

Exploit inefficiencies across platforms, asset classes, or geographies.

Wildcard

Experimental alpha concepts that challenge conventional assumptions.

Structure of a Semester

  • Desk Pitches: Each desk delivers one formal pitch per semester with core idea and hedge.
  • New Member Project: All new members complete an original pitch — any strategy, any asset class.
  • Education: Weekly training covering valuation, modeling, markets, and hedge fund case studies.
  • Career Development: Resume refinement, interview preparation, and mentorship from experienced members.
  • Pitch Competition: Top pitch earns $500 in real cash prize.
  • Club Culture: Internal socials to build a strong, collaborative, and enduring network.
