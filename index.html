Who We Are

New Wave Capital is a community of ambitious, high-performing students rethinking investment education. We welcome both Ross and non-Ross students from finance, STEM, policy, and beyond — united by curiosity and bold analysis.

Whether your path is investment banking, PE, quant, consulting, or something unconventional — we provide the platform to pitch, learn, and lead.

What a Semester Looks Like

  • Members are split into specialized investment desks by strategy.
  • Each desk pitches a core idea and hedge plan once per semester.
  • New members complete an independent pitch of any asset class or method.
  • Weekly education on hedge fund strategy, modeling, and alpha simulation.
  • Semester dinner + culture events to build community and reward performance.
  • Career development: resume reviews, networking, and interview prep.
