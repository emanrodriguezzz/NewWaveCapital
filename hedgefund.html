Our Hedge Fund Experience

New Wave Capital is structured as a simulated hedge fund, giving students real exposure to professional investing workflows. Members are assigned to strategy-focused desks including:

  • Quantitative Investing
  • Macroeconomic & Policy Strategies
  • Long/Short Equity
  • Private Equity and Arbitrage

Each desk is responsible for producing an institutional-grade investment thesis — including a mandatory hedge strategy to mitigate risk — in alignment with real hedge fund practices.

To raise the stakes, we award $500 each semester to the best overall pitch, judged on originality, feasibility, and risk management.
