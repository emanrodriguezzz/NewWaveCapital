A Student-Run Hedge Fund Experience

Members join one of our strategy desks and develop real investment theses, including a required hedge strategy. Each semester culminates in a high-stakes pitch competition, with a $500 prize to the top team.

Quantitative Investing

Statistical models and algorithmic trading strategies derived from structured and unstructured data.

Macroeconomic & Policy Strategies

Positioning driven by global economics, interest rates, policy changes, and geopolitical shifts.

Long/Short Equity

Stock selection and fundamental valuation paired with short positions to hedge sector or market risk.

Private Equity & Arbitrage

Investment theses on private companies and cross-asset arbitrage in public or OTC markets.

Derivatives

Options, futures, and structured products used to hedge or express asymmetric investment views.

Wildcard

Nontraditional pitches: anything from prediction markets to NFTs — built to innovate and challenge norms.
