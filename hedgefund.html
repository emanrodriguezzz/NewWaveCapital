Hedge Fund Strategy Desks

Simulating the real-world structure of a modern hedge fund, New Wave Capital offers students deep experience in applied investing.

Quantitative Investing

Backtested models and signal-driven strategies using statistics and Python to find consistent alpha.

Macroeconomic & Policy

Monetary policy, interest rate cycles, global catalysts, and cross-asset positioning on macro themes.

Long/Short Equity

Company-specific fundamental or technical theses with asymmetric upside and required downside hedging.

Private Equity & Arbitrage

Deal analysis, special situations, mispriced M&A, and multi-asset arbitrage concepts for compounding return.

Derivatives

Creative optionality and tail-risk hedging using volatility surfaces, futures curves, and structured trades.

Wildcard

Unconventional assets—crypto, sports models, machine learning—all welcome with strong rationale.

$500 Pitch Competition

Each desk competes to deliver the strongest investment thesis of the semester. Every pitch must include a clear return profile and a well-thought-out hedge strategy.

A portfolio committee of advanced members will help ensure desk diversification and cross-asset balance—treating the fund like real capital is being allocated.

Any asset class, strategy, or framework is allowed—we value bold thinking and risk-adjusted creativity. Our north star: beat the S&P 500.

The top pitch wins a $500 prize and is recognized as the leading investor of the semester.
